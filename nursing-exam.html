AIIMS NORCET Overview

The AIIMS NORCET (Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test) is a national exam conducted by AIIMS for recruiting Nursing Officers across India’s AIIMS hospitals. It's designed to assess clinical knowledge, patient care, and medical procedures.

Eligibility

Candidates need a B.Sc. Nursing or Diploma in General Nursing and Midwifery with registration in the Nursing Council. Both fresh graduates and experienced nurses are welcome to apply.

Exam Structure and Syllabus

Here, candidates can explore the exam structure, syllabus, and preparation tips. Exam Structure:

  • Format: Computer-based test with multiple-choice questions (MCQs).

  • Duration: 180 minutes.

  • Subjects Covered: Core nursing subjects, general awareness, and basic quantitative skills.
